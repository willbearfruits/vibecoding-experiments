ATARI PUNK CONSOLE PARTS

  • 2 × 555 Timer ICs Salvage from old electronics or any electronics supplier
  • R1: 10kΩ Resistor Literally anywhere that sells electronics
  • R3: 1kΩ Resistor Common in almost all electronic devices
  • R5: 100kΩ Resistor Common in almost all electronic devices
  • C1: 0.01μF Capacitor Salvage from old circuit boards
  • C2: 0.1μF Capacitor Salvage from old circuit boards
  • R4: 100kΩ Potentiometer Old radios, TVs, etc.
  • 9V Battery Clip Dead smoke detectors or electronics stores
  • Audio Jack (1/8" or 1/4") Broken headphones or electronics stores

BUILD NOTES

The Atari Punk Console (or APC) is a classic noise-maker that produces square-wave sounds. It's perfect for beginners and can be the foundation for more complex noise machines.

Experiment with different capacitor values to change the sound range. Smaller capacitors = higher frequencies. You can also add switches to toggle between different capacitor values for more sound options.

WARNING: This will generate harsh tones that might damage speakers at high volumes. Start with low volume and work your way up.

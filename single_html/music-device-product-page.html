Technical Specifications

Discover the innovative technology behind our instruments.

  • Sample Rate 96kHz / 48kHz / 44.1kHz (selectable)
  • Bit Depth 24-bit / 16-bit (selectable)
  • Oscillators 4x digital oscillators with wavetable synthesis
  • Filters Dual multi-mode analog filters with resonance
  • LFOs 6x assignable LFOs with multiple waveforms
  • Effects Reverb, delay, chorus, distortion, bit crusher
  • Noise Generator White, pink, brown, and filtered noise options

  • 2 555 Timer ICs Salvage from old electronics or any electronics supplier
  • R1: 10k Resistor Literally anywhere that sells electronics
  • R3: 1k Resistor Common in almost all electronic devices
  • R5: 100k Resistor Common in almost all electronic devices
  • C1: 0.01F Capacitor Salvage from old circuit boards

  • R4: 100k Potentiometer Old radios, TVs, etc.
  • 9V Battery Clip Dead smoke detectors or electronics stores

FEEDBACK LOOP

Pure audio feedback generated by the Contact Crawler with its output feeding back into its input. The resulting loop is shaped by bandpass filters and distortion to create this unstable, screaming sound.

DEVICE: CONTACT CRAWLER CIRCUIT: FEEDBACK SYSTEM DURATION: 02:22
